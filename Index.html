Problem & Goal

Many UP campus vendors currently serve walk-ins only, causing long queues and poor visibility of preparation times. This demo site shows how menus and pickup times can be centralised and browsed online.

How it works

  1. Choose a vendor and view its menu.
  2. Select items and choose a pickup time slot.
  3. Pay securely with your UP account (demo) and track order status.
